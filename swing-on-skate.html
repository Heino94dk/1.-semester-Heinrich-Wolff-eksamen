Logo design:
This is our school project "dans dans dans". The project was about the dance "swing" where we had make a website for a dance studio. To make the dance swing more exating it was made into swing on skates. In the projekt I helped with the whole process and also did som coding, but the thing i was in charge of was the logo. The logo you see in the beginning is the finsih produkt.

Process of the logo. I started by finding some roller skates that had the colors from our moodboard. Then i found the image you can see underneith by YeoYs Retro. I then cut out the roller skates and made a background that again match the moodboard and lastly I added the text and at first it was not round enough, as it is in the final produkt.
